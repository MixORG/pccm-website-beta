Screening Camps

PCCMâ€™s Breast Health Screening Camps

Orchid's Breast Health Clinic's clinical staff conduct screening camps at various locations such as college campuses, corporate offices, healthcare centers, and underprivileged areas to screen groups for breast cancer. They provide first-hand information about the disease and then conduct clinical breast examinations. If any abnormality is detected, the individuals are called in for further assessment at the Clinic. Early detection contributes to improved outcomes and better overall prognosis.
