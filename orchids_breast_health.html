Orchids Breast Clinic: Committed to Holistic Breast health

Orchids Breast Clinic, an initiative under Prashanti Cancer Care, stands as a comprehensive hub for the management of breast cancer. Led by the pioneer in breast oncoplastic and reconstruction surgery, Dr. C. B. Koppiker, the clinic has played a pivotal role in aiding numerous patients. Since its establishment in 2009, Orchids Breast Clinic has been at the forefront of providing affordable and holistic breast cancer treatment in Pune. Notably, it is the sole standalone breast cancer center in Pune, distinguished by its state-of-the-art infrastructure dedicated to the diagnosis and treatment of breast cancer at affordable rates.Orchids breast clinic was the first to have the 3D tomosynthesis mammography machine along with the first VABB machine in Maharashtra

About Dr. C. B Koppiker

Dr. Chaitanyanand B Koppiker is a Breast Oncosurgeon and the Medical Director at Orchids Breast Health Center. Dr. Koppiker is one of the pioneers of Breast Oncoplasty in India. He is an Honorary Faculty and Asia Convener for Masters in Breast Oncoplasty Program at University of East Anglia and has established the International School of Breast Oncoplasty in collaboration with the senior faculty of the Association of Breast Surgeons, UK and the University of East Anglia.

About Ms. Laleh Busheri

Ms. Laleh Busheri, a compassionate visionary and co-founder of Prashanti Cancer Care Mission, has dedicated herself to making a significant impact in the field of breast cancer care. Her deep empathy towards breast cancer patients has been instrumental in the establishment of Orchids Breast Clinic. She believes in providing specialized and empathetic care for all patients coming to Prashanti.
