Empowering breast health through compassionate care

PCCM is a prominent NGO based in Pune, Maharashtra, founded by the esteemed Dr. C. B Koppiker, a globally recognized Breast Oncologist. The main objective of this NGO is to provide free and affordable breast cancer treatments and rehabilitation to underprivileged breast cancer patients. In addition, it has an active arm for Breast Cancer education, training, and research. Through its multi-pronged activities, viz. Clinical, Academics, Research, and Support Activities, PCCM has been able to serve thousands of patients annually in and around Pune city.

One Voice, One Mission: Beating Breast Cancer

PCCM’s vision is to eradicate the barriers to quality healthcare, especially for underprivileged breast cancer patients, by providing free and affordable treatments and rehabilitation. PCCM aspires to lead the way in breast cancer education, training, and research, fostering a society that is well- informed, empowered, and capable of effectively combating breast cancer.
