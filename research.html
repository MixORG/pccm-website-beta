Advancing Breast Cancer Research at PCCM

Prashanti Cancer Care Mission has collaborated with the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) to form The Centre for Translational Cancer Research (CTCR). The primary objective of CTCR is to facilitate cancer research, training, and education by bringing together researchers and clinicians to find effective solutions for cancer problems relevant to India. It has infrastructure and facilities for training in translational cancer research.Through the Breast Tissue BioBank and Clinical Database CTCR is able to initiate and perform number of projects. The broad heads of the type of projects are shown in the graphical representation. Apart from clinical, translation and patient-centric research, PCCM-CTCR is also very active in capacity building.
