Awareness talks
Awareness initiative by Prashanti

Early detection of breast cancer is a critical step in managing the disease. Therefore, PCCM regularly organizes awareness talks in corporate offices, colleges, and underprivileged areas to raise awareness. Ms. Laleh Busheri, the CEO of PCCM, herself participates in awareness camps where she discusses the signs and symptoms of breast cancer, the importance of early diagnosis & undergoing screening, self-breast examination, and the gold standard treatment in breast cancer.

Screening Camps
PCCMâ€™s Breast Health Screening Camps

Orchid's Breast Health Clinic's clinical staff conduct screening camps at various locations such as college campuses, corporate offices, healthcare centers, and underprivileged areas to screen groups for breast cancer. They provide first-hand information about the disease and then conduct clinical breast examinations. If any abnormality is detected, the individuals are called in for further assessment at the Clinic. Early detection contributes to improved outcomes and better overall prognosis.

Support Group Events
PCCM: A trusted companion in breast cancer journey

Support group events add a touch of positivity to the lives of patients and their families, highlighting PCCM's commitment to addressing the multifaceted challenges posed by breast cancer.

Counselling
Holistic well being for patients

Breast cancer cases in India are increasing, and although medical treatments can relieve physical symptoms, the psychological impact of the disease often goes unnoticed. PCCM's Orchid's Breast Clinic recognizes the importance of holistic care. The clinic's staff offers unwavering support to patients, with doctors addressing medical concerns, physician assistants available to help patients whenever they need assistance, and an onco psychologist providing emotional support to help patients cope with the diagnosis.

Additionally, Orchids Breast Health offers free nutrition and yoga counseling to breast cancer patients. Recognizing that various subtypes of breast cancer may have different nutritional requirements. Our qualified nutrition counselors give personalized nutrition plans to aid patients in recovering from illness. PCCM-Genetic Clinic was established in 2017. It offers complementary Genetic Counseling and also subsidized Genetic Testing for breast cancer patients>

Food Bank
Food Bank for Health

PCCM extends its commitment beyond medical care. The organization actively organizes food donations through its food banks, reaching out to support the nutritional needs of the underprivileged population.
